## 1. Brazilian Brokers

### NuInvest (Nubank)

- **Corretagem (app/home broker):** R$0 (zero) for ações, ETFs, FIIs, BDRs, and options
- **Mesa de Operações:** 0.5% + R$25.21 (minimum R$50) for orders placed via trading desk
- **Custódia:** none
- **Emolumentos B3/CBLC:** charged at B3 standard rates (third-party, not NuInvest)
- **Tax reporting:** Informe de Rendimentos (issued February/March); DARF for renda variável is self-reported by investor
- **URL:** https://nuinvest.com.br

**Best for:** Zero-cost active trading in ações, ETFs, FIIs, BDRs via the app; lowest friction entry point for B3.

### BTG Pactual

- **Corretagem renda variável:** R$4.50/order base rate; regressive tiered structure (per-order cost decreases at higher monthly trading volume)
- **Custódia:** none for individual accounts
- **Renda fixa:** strong desk — CDBs up to 112% CDI available; robust fixed income offering
- **Tax reporting:** Informe de Rendimentos; DARF for renda variável is self-reported by investor
- **URL:** https://investimentos.btgpactual.com

**Best for:** Higher-volume traders who benefit from tiered pricing; investors prioritizing fixed income with institutional-grade rates.

## 2. Key Brazil Tax Note — DARF Self-Reporting

**No Brazilian broker auto-withholds DARF for renda variável.**

Unlike the German system where domestic brokers withhold Abgeltungssteuer automatically, all Brazilian brokers require the investor to:

1. **Calculate monthly gains** for stocks, FIIs, and ETFs sold at a profit
2. **Generate and pay the DARF** (Documento de Arrecadação de Receitas Federais) by the last business day of the month following the sale
3. **Declare annual income** via the Informe de Rendimentos provided by the broker — this covers dividends and other income received, not capital gains

The Informe de Rendimentos is issued by brokers in February/March for the prior tax year. It covers:
- Rendimentos isentos (dividends from ações, FII rendimentos exempt up to limits)
- Rendimentos sujeitos a tributação exclusiva (renda fixa, some FII income)
- Bens e direitos held as of December 31

Capital gains from ações, FIIs, and ETFs are NOT included in the Informe — they must be tracked and declared by the investor in the monthly DARF and in the annual Declaração de IRPF (Programa GCAP).
