1. **Steuerklassen I-VI** (DETAX-01 — write fresh, not in skill):
   - Table with all 6 classes: I (single/divorced/widowed), II (single parent, Entlastungsbetrag ~4,260 EUR), III (married higher earner, paired with V), IV (married equal earners), IV with factor (married moderate split), V (married lower earner, paired with III), VI (second job, no Freibetrag)
   - Explicit note: "Steuerklassen affect employment income withholding only. Capital gains taxed at flat 25% Abgeltungssteuer regardless of class. However, class affects marginal rate which determines Guenstigerpruefung eligibility."
   - Recommendation logic: single -> I, single parent -> II, married -> III/V if large income gap else IV/IV-factor

2. **Abgeltungssteuer** (DETAX-05 — copy from skill):
   - Flat rate: 25%
   - Plus Solidaritaetszuschlag: 5.5% of 25% = 1.375%
   - Effective rate without church tax: 26.375%
   - With church tax (8%): 27.819%, with church tax (9%): 27.995%
   - Guenstigerpruefung: if marginal rate < 26.375%, file Anlage KAP to claim refund
   - Anlage KAP line mapping if available in skill

3. **Sparerpauschbetrag** (DETAX-02 — copy from skill):
   - Single: 1,000 EUR, Married: 2,000 EUR (since tax year 2023, NOT the old 801 EUR)
   - Freistellungsauftrag: filed per broker, sum must not exceed allowance
   - Warning: sum > allowance is illegal under section 44a EStG, triggers Finanzamt query
   - Cross-broker strategy: allocate proportionally to expected capital income per broker

4. **Teilfreistellung** (DETAX-04 — copy from skill):
   - Table: Equity funds 30%, Mixed funds 15%, Bond funds 0%, Real estate funds (domestic) 60%, Real estate funds (foreign) 80%
   - Explicit note: "Teilfreistellung applies to funds (ETFs, investment funds) ONLY — not to dividends or gains from individual stocks"
   - Applied to both distributions and capital gains from fund sales

5. **Vorabpauschale** (DETAX-03 — copy from skill):
   - Formula: Basisertrag = Fund value Jan 1 x Basiszins x 0.70
   - Vorabpauschale = min(Basisertrag, actual fund gain in year)
   - After Teilfreistellung (equity): Vorabpauschale x 0.70
   - Tax = after_teilfreistellung x effective_rate (26.375% or 27.995%)
   - Basiszins values: 2025: 2.29%, 2026: 3.20% (note: "verify 2026 against current BMF/Bundesbank publication")
   - Charged by broker in January for prior year — keep cash buffer in account
   - Offset against Sparerpauschbetrag first

6. **Tax Calendar**:
   - January: Vorabpauschale charged by broker
   - Ongoing: Freistellungsauftrag adjustments
   - December: Tax-loss harvesting window (note: "detailed tax-loss harvesting is a future feature")
   - Annual: Anlage KAP filing if Guenstigerpruefung or foreign broker used

7. **Important Notes**:
   - Pre-2018 ETF taxation rules are fully superseded by InvStG 2018 — do not reference
   - 801 EUR Sparerpauschbetrag is outdated since 2023 — always use 1,000/2,000 EUR
   - Tax-loss harvesting mentioned briefly with note it is deferred to v2
