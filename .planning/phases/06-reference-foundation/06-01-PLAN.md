SECTION 2: Adjusted 50/30/20 Rule (per D-02)

| Category | DE Range | BR Range | Notes |
|----------|----------|----------|-------|

SECTION 3: Emergency Fund Threshold (per D-03)

SECTION 4: Debt-to-Income Ratio

- Includes: mortgage payments, consumer credit, car finance, student loans
- Excludes: mandatory social contributions (already netted in denominator)

TITLE: `# Tax Efficiency and Allocation Scoring Rules`

STALENESS NOTICE (blockquote):
  "Tax year notice: This document reflects scoring thresholds for tax year 2025.
   Verify underlying tax rules against official sources before using for a different tax year."

INTRO PARAGRAPH: State that this doc defines traffic-light (green/yellow/red) thresholds per dimension per country (D-05, D-06, D-07). It does NOT restate tax rules — it references germany/tax-investment.md and brazil/tax-investment.md for underlying figures. Each dimension produces a color indicator AND an absolute euro/real gap amount.

## Germany Dimensions

### TAX-01: Sparerpauschbetrag Usage
- Source: germany/tax-investment.md Section 3 — EUR 1,000 (single) / EUR 2,000 (married)
- Green: >= 90% utilized (unused less than EUR 100 single / EUR 200 married)
- Yellow: 50-89% utilized (unused EUR 100-500 single / EUR 200-1,000 married)
- Red: < 50% utilized (unused greater than EUR 500 single / EUR 1,000 married)
- Gap formula: unused_allowance = limit - sum_of_freistellungsauftrag_across_all_brokers
- Tax cost of gap: unused x 26.375% (or 27.819%/27.995% with church tax)

### TAX-03: Vorabpauschale Readiness
- Source: germany/tax-investment.md Section 5 — formula: Fund value x Basiszins x 0.70
- Green: Cash buffer >= 110% of estimated Vorabpauschale tax due in January
- Yellow: Cash buffer 80-109% of estimated Vorabpauschale
- Red: Cash buffer < 80% (risk of forced unit sale)
- Fallback when current-year Basiszins unavailable: use prior-year Basiszins with "ESTIMATED — verify current Basiszins from BMF" label; flag confidence as MEDIUM

### ALLOC-01: Investment Rate (DE)
- Green: investment rate >= 20% of net-after-mandatory
- Yellow: 15-19% (meets minimum per D-04, below aspirational)
- Red: < 15% of net-after-mandatory

### ALLOC-02: Emergency Fund (DE)
- Green: liquid savings >= 6 months expenses
- Yellow: 3-5 months (adequate but below cross-border target per D-03)
- Red: < 3 months

## Brazil Dimensions

### TAX-02: DARF Compliance
- Source: brazil/tax-investment.md Section 2
- Green: all months with taxable gains had DARF filed and paid on time
- Yellow: DARF obligations present but status unknown (no data in profile)
- Red: known gap month (taxable gain recorded, no payment confirmed) — flag with penalty estimate

### TAX-02: PGBL Deduction Utilization
- Source: brazil/tax-investment.md Section 1 (PGBL/VGBL rules)
- Applies only to users with declaracao: completa
- Green: PGBL contribution >= 12% of gross income (maximum deductible limit)
- Yellow: PGBL contribution 6-11% of gross
- Red: < 6% contribution or no PGBL (and no documented reason such as VGBL preference)

### ALLOC-01: Investment Rate (BR)
- Green: investment rate >= 15% of net-after-mandatory (including FGTS)
- Yellow: 10-14% (meets minimum)
- Red: < 10% of net-after-mandatory
- Note: FGTS contributions count toward total — read from profile countries.brazil.fgts_contribution

### ALLOC-02: Emergency Fund (BR)
- Same thresholds as DE: Green >= 6 months, Yellow 3-5 months, Red < 3 months
- BR liquid assets: poupanca, conta corrente, CDB de liquidez diaria, LCI/LCA with daily liquidity
